Uploading is simple: drag-and-drop or click to select files. Encryption happens automatically in your browser.

### Step-by-Step Upload

1. Log in to the dashboard.
2. Click the **Upload** button (or drag files directly onto the page).

    ![Upload button location](/img/cloud/upload-button.png)

3. Select your file(s) or folder (multiple uploads supported).
4. Choose your **encryption mode** (default: AES-256-GCM):

    ![Encryption mode selection](/img/cloud/encryption-mode.png)

6. Click **Encrypt & Upload**.

    ![Uploaded file confirmation](/img/cloud/uploaded-file.png)
